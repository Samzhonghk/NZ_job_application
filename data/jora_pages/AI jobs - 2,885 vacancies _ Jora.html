          Help businesses unlock the power of data and AI

          We're looking for a Data & AI Specialist who is passionate about turning information into insight and helping organisations achieve better outcomes through data, analytics, automation, and artificial intelligence.

          This is an exciting opportunity to work across a diverse range of clients, delivering Data and AI as a Service. You'll combine technical expertise with business problem-solving to help organisations make smarter decisions, improve efficiency, and embrace emerging technologies with confidence. If you're excited about helping businesseDas unlock the value of their data and embrace the possibilities of AI, we'd love to hear from you.

          About Us

          At Halo IT (A subsidiary of Voyager Internet), we're passionate about helping businesses transform the way they work through technology, data, and innovation. As part of the Internet Brands group of companies, we deliver cutting-edge MSP and MSSP solutions to organisations across New Zealand.

          Our team thrives on curiosity, collaboration, and continuous improvement. We work closely with our clients to solve real-world business challenges using modern technologies, data-driven insights, and practical AI solutions.

          At Halo IT, you'll have the opportunity to work with a broad range of industries, technologies, and emerging AI capabilities while being supported by a team that values learning, innovation, and professional growth.

          What Will You Do?

          As our Data & AI Specialist, you'll play a key role in helping clients turn fragmented and often complex data into meaningful insights, reporting solutions, and practical AI-powered capabilities.

          This is a hands-on, client-facing role focused on delivering real business outcomes. You'll work across a variety of client environments, connecting data from multiple systems, building reusable datasets, developing dashboards and reporting solutions, and enabling AI tools that help organisations work smarter.

          You'll collaborate with both technical and non-technical stakeholders to understand business requirements, design solutions, and continuously improve outcomes through data and AI.

          A major focus of the role will be creating clean, structured, AI-ready datasets and implementing repeatable solutions that can be leveraged across multiple clients.

          For more details, please request a copy of the position description.

          About You

          This role would suit someone who enjoys solving real-world business problems through data, analytics, and AI.

          You are naturally curious, enjoy learning new technologies, and thrive in environments where every client presents a different challenge. You have strong technical foundations in data and analytics, combined with the ability to communicate effectively with both technical and business audiences.

          Most importantly, you're passionate about data, excited by the opportunities AI presents, and motivated by helping organizations work smarter.

          What You'll Bring

          Essential Experience
          • Experience working with data through study, projects, internships, or commercial environments
          • Experience cleaning, transforming, and structuring data for analysis and reporting
          • Experience using AI tools and internal datasets to solve business problems
          • Understanding of SQL, database structures, data lakes, and data modelling concepts
          • Experience with ETL/ELT tools, BI platforms, and AI data solutions
          • Experience working within Agile delivery environments
          Essential Skills
          • Data Analysis and Reporting
          • Data Integration and Pipeline Development
          • Power BI (or equivalent BI platform)
          • SQL and Data Modelling
          • AI Agents and AI Enablement Technologies
          • Strong stakeholder engagement and communication skills
          Qualifications
          • Qualification in Data Analytics, Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related discipline
          • Power BI Certification or equivalent experience
          • Business Analysis qualification
          • Data Analytics qualification
          Desirable:
          • Project Management qualification
          • Change Management qualification

          A Career at Halo IT Offers

          • Ongoing support for professional development and certifications
          • Exposure to innovative AI and Digital Transformation projects
          • Opportunities to work with a diverse range of clients and industries
          • Flexible working arrangements (where applicable)
          • Paid Birthday Leave and Long Service Leave
          • Social club events and team activities
          • Free Internet
          • Friends and Family discounts on Internet Brands services
          • A collaborative and supportive team culture

          How to Apply

          If you're excited by the opportunity to work at the intersection of Data, AI, and Digital Transformation, we'd love to hear from you. Please apply through our recruitment platform using the link below and include your CV and a cover letter outlining your experience and interest in the role.

          https://voyager.bamboohr.com/careers/161. Applications directly in seek will not be considered.

          Applicants must have the legal right to work in New Zealand.

          No recruitment agencies please.

          Pre-Employment Checks

          Halo IT requires all successful applicants to complete a Ministry of Justice criminal records check prior to employment.
